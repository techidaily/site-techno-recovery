Unlocking EPUB Content on Your Kindle Paperwhite: Simple Steps for Enjoyment

How to Read EPUB on Kindle Paperwhite

Posted by Ada Wang on 3/13/2024 11:26:28 AM.

5.0 (21 comments)

Many Kindle users are searching for solutions to read epub on Kindle Paperwhite, because Kindle device doesn’t support EPUB natively. Fortunately we’ve found solutions to reading epub on kindle Paperwhite.

  • Method 1. Send epub to Kindle Paperwhite online
  • Method 2. Convert EPUB to Kindle Supported Format
  • Method 3. Jailbreak Kindle Paperwhite.

Is EPUB compatible with Kindle?

--Kindle Paperwhite only supports reading books purchased from Amazon Kindle store. And these Kindle books are in their exclusive eBook formats, such as kfx, mobi, azw3, azw, etc. Since epub is not Kindle supported ebook format, it cannot be detected in Kindle devices even if it has been transferred to Kindle via usb cable.

--Most of the epub books, such as the books purchased Google Play Books, Kobo, Nook, are protected with DRM protection, which prevent us reading epub files on non-supported readers, such as Kindle paperwhite.

Having found the reasons, the solutions will be easy to find. Since there are limitations between the device and the ebook format, why not change the device’s property or the book file’s format?

Method 1. Send ePub to Kindle Paperwhite Online

Amazon now allows us to directly send ePub format books to Kindle by web.

Note: The epub you send to kindle should be DRM-free epub files. If your epub files are protected by drm, please skip tomethod 2 to remove drm from epub books first.

Step 1. Go to Amazon official Send to Kindle Online page (make sure that you already logged into your Amazon account).

Step 2. Click on the yellow button “Select files from device” to upload an ePub file.

Step 3. Select an ePub and add to your library, send.

Step 4. Wait for the ePub file to be sent to Kindle.

Wait until the ePub has been sent across. Once it says “In Library”, you can pick up your kindle to sync and download the newly received file.

1. This method only works for drm free ePub files. If your downloaded ePub books are with drm, please remove ePub drm first.

2. It’s likely that the sent ePub files will be with the format displaying issues on your Kindle device.

Method 2. Convert EPUB to Kindle Supported Format

To read ePub on Kindle Paperwhite, the best and simplest way is ebook format conversion. As we all know that Kindle only reads kindle format such as Mobi/AZW3, so how about converting epub to kindle format?

How to convert epub to Kindle ebook format? Kindle ebook converter is a must-have tool. I’m going to introduce 2 effective tools.

Tool 1. Calibre

As an ebook fan, you must have heard of Calibre. This is a free ebook management tool, which helps you convert ebooks from one format to another. So you can use it to convert your epub files to Kindle format.

Download Calibre , and launch it. Then add your downloaded epub books, select them and click on “Convert books” icon. Choose Mobi/AZW3 as your output format.

Notice: To convert DRM-protected EPUB books, please need to install Calibre DRM Removal plugins .

Tool 2. Epubor Ultimate-Highly Recommended

Epubor Ultimate is the all-in-one ebook DRM Removal and eBook Converter which can help you remove ebook DRM and convert ebook format at once. What’s more, this tool also supports batch conversion. Convert your books in bulk with ease and save your time.

Download Epubor Ultimate for free

Just download the Epubor EPUB DRM Removal –Epubor Ultimate to your computer. Then add epub books to the software. BothDRM-free and DRM-protected epub books are supported perfectly. Then select kindle Mobi or AZW3 as the the target output format, click on “Convert to Mobi” button.

Notice:

1. For DRM-protected epub books, please make sure you can read epub on Adobe Digital Editions. Follow the guide How to Remove DRM from EPUB to learn the details.

2. For some Kobo eBooks they are usually in KEPUB format but not the common ePub, but you still can use Epubor Ultimate to convert kepub to Kindle support format .

After converting your ePub books to Kindle format, transfer the converted files in Kindle-compatible format to your Kindle via a USB cable.

Now you can read epub on Kindle Paperwhite smoothly.

Jailbreaking Kindle Paperwhite is more suitable to those people who don’t want to use Kindle’s restricted services. If you are the person who prefer to Android system rather than Kindle, you can try this solution.

Note:
You will take high risk to use this method. Please back up everything before jailbreaking your Kindle Paperwhite.

Step 2. Install epub reader on new Kindle Paperwhite

After successful jailbreaking, you need to install epub reader app to help you open epub books (it means you don’t have to convert epub to mobi format).

KOReader --Originally created for usage on Kindle e-ink reader, started as Kindle PDF Viewer. But now it supports epub too. When you open an epub file, it will turn this format book to pdf layout. Please follow the KOReader user guide to know how to.

2. If your ePub books are protected by DRM, you need to strip the DRM first and then send them to Kindle. This post introduces two tools: Calibre and Epubor Ultimate. To be honest, if you are not tight on budget, Epubor Ultimate is a perfect choice as it can remove DRM from ePub books and convert them to Kindle-supported formats.
