##  How to Delete Suggested Users From Instagram 

 Instagram tries to be helpful by connecting you with users you may want to follow based on factors like mutual friends and phone contacts. Here's how to remove a suggested profile if you're not interested in following the person:

1. In the Instagram app, scroll through your feed until you see a horizontal list of suggested users labeled**Suggestions for you** . It will likely appear close to the top of your feed, after the first or second most recent post.
2. Select the**X** in the top right corner of any suggested user box. The suggestion will disappear immediately.  
 Instagram will not ask you to confirm that you want to delete the suggestion, so be sure you really don't want to be reminded to follow them. You can't undo this, although you can still search for the person later since this is not the same as[ blocking the profile](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-block-someone-on-instagram-4169853) .
3. Alternatively, to speed up the deletion process, select**See all** above the list of suggested users. Here, you can remove several suggestions in quick succession.

This only works from the website, not the app.

 FAQ

* How do I clear Instagram search suggestions when typing?  
 There is no specific way to remove the Instagram feature of suggesting search results when typing in the search bar. Even after clearing your search history, Instagram will make suggestions, some of which may be based on your past activity.
* How do I appear on someone's Instagram suggestions?  
 There's no definitive method for appearing on someone's Instagram suggestions. To increase your chances of appearing, use relevant hashtags, follow similar accounts, and post high-quality content. Also, ensure you have a[ public Instagram account](https://www.lifewire.com/make-instagram-account-private-3485850) so everyone can see your posts, and confirm that account suggestions are turned on.
* How do I clear the Instagram cache?  
 To completely clear your Instagram cache on an iPhone, you'll need to delete and reinstall the app. On an Android, go to**Settings** \>**Apps** \>**See all \[#\] apps** \>**Instagram** \>**Storage & cache** . Tap**Clear cache** .
