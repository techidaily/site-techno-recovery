## Top 8 Must-Play Mobile Apps: Ultimate Gaming List

Close 

 These days, there are tons of excellent mobile games to play, whether it's a first-person shooter, a strategy game, or something built from the ground up for mobile, like_Subway Surfers_ . Regardless of what genres you're into or what mobile device you've got, these are the top mobile games out there that you can play right now in 2024.

of 08

## Subway Surfers 

![Subway Surfer game on iPhone with character moving horizontally, rolling, and jumping.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/P8xS0bM39rXRhBAP-lZjJljJzeE=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/how-to-play-subway-surfers-01-9e7d863a907847009022a713fbea706e.jpg) 

 What We Like

* Addictive gameplay loop that's easy to jump into at any time for however long.
* Engaging, colorful art style that makes speeding through maps a joy.

 What We Don't Like

* Not the deepest experience out there and can get repetitive as a result.

_[ Subway Surfers](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-play-subway-surfers-8584055)_  is one of the most popular mobile games in the world for good reason: its crunchy, fast-paced gameplay makes it incredibly hard to put down, and with tons of different things to unlock alongside constant updates, it's one of the best all-around packages in the world of mobile games.

**Download for** :

[ Android ](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.kiloo.subwaysurf&hl=en%5FUS&gl=US) 

[ iOS ](https://apps.apple.com/tr/app/subway-surfers/id512939461)

## Monopoly Go 

![Three images of the Monopoly Go video game with Album, Swinging Notes sticker album, and Stage Dream sticker highlighted.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/OKVQJcILcbbXRJZ_BegrUUq9QOk=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/how-to-send-stars-monopoly-go-02-6ea41c585c174fe9886ac56aa3c8c481.jpg) 

 What We Like

* Exciting evolution of the core_Monopoly_ format into a new, catchy game.
* Casual enough gameplay to make it the perfect time waster if you have a couple of minutes to spare.

 What We Don't Like

* Bombards you with ads and things to buy.
* Relatively little interactivity.

_Monopoly Go_ is a fairly new mobile superstar, but it goes way beyond its board game roots. Roll your way around[ the classic board](https://www.lifewire.com/number-of-boards-monopoly-go-8611553) , building landmarks as you go. Unlock tons of cosmetics, participate in events, and enjoy some truly excellent sound design in this novel take on the historic game.

**Download for** :

[ Android ](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.scopely.monopolygo&hl=en%5FUS&gl=US) 

[ iOS ](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/monopoly-go/id1621328561)

## Pokémon Go 

![Pokemon GO video game on Android tablet.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/cyoM_MmWiMF413JDGyHHx3_8Ux4=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/12-best-tablet-games-for-android-tablets-03-8e9d6f3571504872a2cdf828848351d2.jpg) 

 What We Like

* Encourages you to actually go outside and get your body moving.
* Novel AR design coupled with RPG-lite mechanics makes for a satisfying gameplay loop.

 What We Don't Like

* Not much depth, which can get repetitive over time or longer play sessions.
* A lot of its charm is lost if you aren't a fan already.

_Pokémon Go_ has been a top mobile game for a long time, and that comes down to its unique augmented-reality design that makes going out into the real world and catching Pokémon,[ fighting gym leaders](https://www.lifewire.com/gain-xp-and-level-up-fast-in-pokemon-go-5074937) , and battling other trainers a real blast. Plus, it's some great incentive to actually get some exercise, too.

**Download for** :

[ Android ](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nianticlabs.pokemongo&hl=en%5FUS&gl=US) 

[ iOS ](https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/pok%C3%A9mon-go/id1094591345)

## Call of Duty: Mobile 

![Call of Duty: Mobile](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/9Y5RP9tqgpWT_0VmzBLAQHlsZnA=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/COD-MOBILE-PREVIEW_TOUT_NEW-36cf181bb15f467597e45443c575f221.jpg) 

Activision

 What We Like

* Intense, action-packed first-person shooter gameplay.
* Lots of classic maps and weapons to play with.
* Regular updates, lots of things to unlock.

 What We Don't Like

* Not the most competitive experience compared to the mainline games.
* Touchscreen controls can be fairly clunky.

_Call of Duty: Mobile_ is the premiere first-person shooter on phones and tablets. With a huge catalog of maps and guns, years of updates and expansions, and lots of stuff to level up/unlock, it's tough to beat if you're looking for a shooter on your smartphone. Plus, controller support is excellent.

**Download for** :

[ Android ](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.activision.callofduty.shooter&hl=en%5FUS&gl=US) 

[ iOS ](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/call-of-duty-mobile/id1287282214)

## Genshin Impact 

![Genshin Impact promotional screenshot](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/0Ri57oSpSFeWdbXSKtsfRSxBaMQ=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/genshinimpact-f6eb1da6eabf4d30a9e8f088871ea6a7.jpg) 

Cognosphere Pte Ltd

 What We Like

* Gorgeous, big budget open world action-RPG with lots of content.
* Can pick up where you left off on console or PC.
* Many different characters you can unlock and try out.

 What We Don't Like

* Gacha monetization can be expensive and disheartening.
* Writing quality leaves something to be desired.

_Genshin Impact_ is a team-based action RPG set in a huge, anime-inspired open world packed with gorgeous artwork and music. There are tons of quests to complete, areas to explore, characters to unlock, and new content gets added all the time. Plus, you can play on console and PC, too, when you get back home.

**Download for** :

[ Android ](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.miHoYo.GenshinImpact&hl=en%5FUS&gl=US) 

[ iOS ](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/genshin-impact/id1517783697)

## Bloons TD 6 

![Bloons TD 6](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/lSo8gb69tJj5nJRb3wyOmu0Fp7k=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/bloonstd6-2decb61d81b5486785ae99cf563521b6.jpg) 

Ninja Kiwi

 What We Like

* Some of the best tower defense gameplay on any platform.
* Tons of maps, different difficulties, and a healthy variety of modes.
* Lots to upgrade and unlock as well as some serious potential for long-term progression.

 What We Don't Like

* Can feel grindy at times if you don't want to spend money.
* Performance can be an issue on older devices.

_Bloons TD 6_ is one of the most respected, beloved[ tower-defense games](https://www.lifewire.com/best-ipad-strategy-games-1994190) of all time. With surprisingly deep gameplay, an enormous amount of maps and modes, and enough to unlock that you'll be coming back for more, this is undoubtedly the crown jewel of the long-running_Bloons_ series.

## Hearthstone

 Hearthstone: Heroes of Warcraft. © Blizzard 

 What We Like

* The best digital card game you can play on your phone.
* Continuous updates and expansions to keep the game feeling fresh.
* Deep levels of strategy at higher levels of play.

 What We Don't Like

* Balance can be get thrown out of whack with new cards.
* Can be overwhelming starting off as a new player.

_Hearthstone_ is Blizzard's_Warcraft_ [ digital card game](https://www.lifewire.com/top-collectible-card-games-ipad-1994142) , and it's been around long enough to have amassed an impressive amount of cards, modes, and heroes to try out. Plus, climbing the competitive ladder can be a seriously challenging and satisfying experience, and you never have to wait long for new cards to come out.
