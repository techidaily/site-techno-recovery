###  What to Know

* Open the app store and check for updates. If there's one for Facebook, tap**Update** .
* Open the app and go to**menu** \>**Settings & privacy** \>**Dark mode** . Tap**On** to enable dark mode for Facebook.
* Alternatively, if your phone is already set up for dark mode, you can choose the**system** option.

 This article explains how to get dark mode back in the Facebook app. Instructions apply to iOS and Android.  

##  How to Get Dark Mode Back in the Facebook App 

 An outdated app is the most common reason you can't turn on dark mode for Facebook. Follow these steps to get it back:  

 These screenshots were made from an Android device, but the steps are the same for iPhone.

1. Close Facebook by swiping it off the screen.
2. Update the app. This works similarly on both platforms: Android \[[ Update Android Apps](https://www.lifewire.com/update-apps-on-android-7106106) \] and iOS \[[ Update iPhone Apps](https://www.lifewire.com/ways-to-keep-iphone-apps-to-date-2000645) \].
3. Open Facebook and tap the**menu** button (your profile picture).
4. Go to**Settings & privacy** \>**Dark mode** .
5. Tap**On** to switch Facebook to dark mode.

##  Why Is Facebook Dark Mode Not Working? 

 The Facebook app has its own version of dark mode in addition to an option to respect the current system settings. If it isn't working, there are a few possible causes:

* You're using an old version of the app.
* The app has glitched.
* The dark mode toggle is switched off.
* Dark mode matches your system settings, which_aren't_ set to dark mode.

 FAQ

* Why did Facebook remove dark mode?  
 Facebook has not removed support for dark mode. Some users have reported the feature missing, but it has always been a glitch that Facebook fixed with an update either on the server side or to the app itself.
* How do I turn on dark mode on the Facebook website?  
 First, click your profile picture in the upper-right corner. Then, go to**Settings & privacy** \>**Settings** \>**Accessibility** \>**View** (next to**Display** ). Tap the**Off** button, then choose**On**  to turn dark mode on.
