## Unlock Fun for Your Little Ones: A Curated List of Six Fantastic Websites with FREE Children's Movie Streaming Options

Close 

 There's no need to pay for the movies the kids want to see because there are hundreds of free kids movies online. These free movies for kids can all be found at[ websites where it's legal to watch movies](https://www.lifewire.com/watch-free-movies-online-1356647) . They all have separate areas where children can go and catch up on the latest movies as well as some of their favorite free TV shows.

 There's a wide variety of free kids movies online that include classics from your childhood as well as the big blockbuster hits of the past few years. These sites compile movies that are great for children of all ages as well as some that the whole family will enjoy.

 If your kids like to watch movies on their tablet, consider a[ free movie app](https://www.lifewire.com/free-movie-streaming-apps-1357452) . Also be sure to check out how to[ use Redbox to get free movies](https://www.lifewire.com/free-redbox-codes-1356432) for your kids.

## [ Yidio](https://www.lifewire.com/yidio-s-free-streaming-movies-and-tv-shows-1357437) 

![Free kids and family movies at yidio](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/KAhQSxPnL4xbiUD_UraERu3s1mI=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/yidio-free-kids-movies-df352c0e37e84a578720f969f9996cef.png) 

 What We Like

* Larger collection than other sites.
* No sign-up required.
* Pulls from other streaming services.

 What We Don't Like

* A lot of movies listed are not free to watch.
* Video quality is decent yet inconsistent.

 Yidio searches all the free movie websites and puts lists together so that all the movies are in one place, including free movies for kids. To view these titles, select**Genre** \>**Kids and Family** .

 Inside this movie section, you can sort by popularity and recently added selections, and filter the kids movies by rating such as G, PG, and PG-13.

 Some great free kids movies we've spotted here include _Escape From Planet Earth, Hoodwinked Too!, Spirit of the Forest, Cop Dog, The Snowman, Mia and the Migoo, Finding Rin Tin Tin, Lion of Oz_ , and_Soccer Mom._

## [ Tubi](https://www.lifewire.com/free-online-tubi-tv-1357455) 

![tubi free kids movies](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/DpzyylmWbWPp_vebI3HRxbXHRbc=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/tubi-free-kids-movies-31f3027a0cb042d4bbba83cf5c374662.png) 

 What We Like

* "Only Free on Tubi" section has site-exclusive titles.
* Adjust video quality to suit your internet connection.

 What We Don't Like

* Streaming service not available in every country.
* The same ads play over and over again.

 Tubi has a section for family movies and kids' shows, and even an entirely separate area of the site called Tubi Kids. Although you can't organize the films by rating or popularity, a single, full page of free kids' movies makes it easy to browse.

 _The Happy Elf, All Dogs Go to Heaven, Igor, The Secret of Nimh, Little Bear Movie, The Amazing Feats of Young Hercules, Eloise: Eloise Goes to School,_ and_Cirque Du Soleil: Worlds Away_ are just some of the movies we've seen available at Tubi.

 Tubi has free TV shows for kids, too, including _Family Game Night_ ,_Transformers_ ,_Sabrina_ ,_Babar_ ,_Rescue Heroes_ , and_G.I. Joe_ .

[ Visit Tubi ](https://tubitv.com/category/family%5Fmovies)

## [ Fandango at Home](https://www.lifewire.com/vudu-adds-tv-shows-on-demand-1847424) 

![free kids movies at Fandango at Home](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/LWsSluq6l3KDyI-nSuRSTsFiibo=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/vudu-free-kids-movies-01aba9a3ce8a4a878268e5fbd242bdd2.png) 

 What We Like

* Hundreds of free movies.
* Lets you buy and rent new movies.

 What We Don't Like

* You have to watch ads.
* Can't sort the kids movies by popularity.
* Requires a free user account.

 Fandango at Home is an excellent movie streaming site with a section dedicated to children called**Family & Kids** . It has hundreds of movies you can stream absolutely free; you just have to watch ads to support the site.

 A few of the free movies for kids that you can stream at Fandango at Home include_Charlotte's Web, Happy Feet, Dennis the Menace, Daddy Day Camp,_ and_Dinosaur Island._

## [ Pluto TV](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-pluto-tv-4774389) 

![nick jr on pluto tv](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/YyWItH-_zICvU5jC9m2eMgXaRAQ=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/pluto-tv-kids-abeda68d9d0d40e1b57e8a4f571e9726.png) 

 What We Like

* Convenient TV-like channel guide interface.
* Rewind shows.

 What We Don't Like

* Can be hard to navigate the interface.

 Kids can watch free movies all day long with Pluto TV. It's 100 percent free and works on a computer, smart TV, and mobile device. There's even a Pluto TV Kids mode you can use if you make a user account.

 Pluto TV streams kids-related movies and shows on various channels. There are also[ on-demand kids movies](https://pluto.tv/en/on-demand/category/5b92b6b7ba333d799e52d590/view-all) (look for**Kids Movie Club** ) that you can stream any time you want without waiting for something you like to show up on that channel.

 Pluto TV has been known to play kids movies like_Alpha And Omega_ movies,_Jungle Girl and the Lost Island of Dinosaurs_ ,_Sea Level_ ,_Agent Cody Banks_ ,_The Invincible Iron Man_ ,_Planet Hulk_ ,_Almighty Thor_ , and_Igor_ .

 Visit the link below to find an entire section of TV channels for kids, including TV NickJr., Mister Rogers' Neighborhood, Nickelodeon Totally Turtles, Lego Kids TV, and more.

[ Visit Pluto TV ](https://pluto.tv/live-tv/)

## [ Sling Freestream](https://watch.sling.com/dashboard/on%5Fdemand/on%5Fdemand%5Fkids) 

![sling tv free kids movies](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/ukQ6wv1H0_oJFOq2ehLDW11FzQA=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/sling-tv-kids-movies-e1274b0f9ac548d7bfefade8d584d5fb.png) 

 What We Like

* No user account needed.
* All the kids titles are on one page.

 What We Don't Like

* Relatively few titles.
* Movies are mixed with TV shows.

 Sling Freestream is a section of the[ Sling TV](https://www.lifewire.com/sling-tv-4154536) service that includes just free movies and TV shows. We like it because you don't need to make an account, so you can jump right in and start watching free stuff immediately.

 Compared to other free movie sites, however, this one has an extremely small collection, which is why it's at the bottom of this list. There are three sections the movies are categorized in: Kids' Animation, Tweens, and Tiny Tots.

 Here are some examples of movies you can watch: _Two Buddies and a Badger, The Jungle Bunch, Blinky Bill: The Movie, Princess Emmy, Quackerz_ , and_A Monster In Paris_ .
