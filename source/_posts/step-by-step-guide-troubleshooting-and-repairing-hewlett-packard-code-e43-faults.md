* How do I connect my AirPods to a Mac?

* How do I connect AirPods to a Chromebook?  
 To[connect AirPods to a Chromebook](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-connect-airpods-to-chromebook-4707152) , select**Menu** on the Chromebook, then select**Bluetooth** and turn on the Bluetooth connection.
