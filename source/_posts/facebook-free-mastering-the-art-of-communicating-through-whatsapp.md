###  What to Know

* You must have a Facebook password to use Messenger. But, you can pause/deactivate Facebook and keep Messenger.
* If you've already deactivated your Facebook, simply log in to Messenger. Facebook will remain disabled.
* If you're new to Messenger, make a Facebook account first,[ deactivate Facebook](https://www.lifewire.com/deactivate-facebook-5072237) , and then sign in to Messenger.

 This article covers how you can stop using Facebook but keep using Messenger. We'll go over how to get Messenger back if you've already deactivated Facebook and how to sign up for a new Messenger account if you don't yet have Facebook.

##  How to Make a New Messenger Account Without Facebook 

 Did you permanently delete Facebook or have you never had a Facebook account? It's easy to make a Messenger account because you get one automatically when you create a Facebook account. Then, you can turn off Facebook and keep using Messenger. Here's how:

1. [ Make a Facebook account](https://www.lifewire.com/tutorial-how-facebook-works-2654610) . You can do this through the website, the Messenger app, or the Facebook app. Select**Create new account** and then follow the on-screen steps.

 FAQ

* Can you contact someone on Messenger without being friends?  
 Yes, you can send a message to someone on Facebook Messenger if you're not friends. However, your message may arrive in their message requests, and they will only see it if they check there. To[ add phone contacts to Messenger](https://www.lifewire.com/add-anyone-to-facebook-messenger-4156519) , select the**People** tab and then tap the**contacts** button.
* How many Messenger accounts can you have?  
 You can add up to five Messenger accounts provided they follow[ Facebook's name policies](https://www.facebook.com/help/112146705538576/) and[ Community Standards](https://transparency.meta.com/policies/community-standards/) . To add a Messenger account in the app, open the menu, tap your profile picture, and then choose**Add account** .
* Can people find me on Messenger without Facebook?  
 Yes. People can search for you by name, email address, or phone number in the Messenger app. To control your privacy settings in the app, tap the menu and then tap the**settings** icon. Go to**Privacy & safety** \>**Message delivery** and choose who can send you message requests.
