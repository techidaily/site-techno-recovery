 This guide only covers the_X-Men_ and mutant films produced by 20th Century Fox. The franchise is unconnected to the[Marvel Cinematic Universe, which is also available to stream](https://www.lifewire.com/watch-marvel-movies-in-order-online-5077381) , and consists of the original_X-Men_ trilogy, a prequel_X-Men Beginnings_ series, an informal trilogy of_Wolverine_ films, two_Deadpool_ films, and a horror spin-off called_The New Mutant_ s.
