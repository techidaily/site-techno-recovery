## Navigate Through Free Online AI Education: Lifewire's Selection of 15 Self-Paced Courses

Close 

 Even though[ AI](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-artificial-intelligence-5119206) is everywhere, don't be dismayed if you still find it confusing. It's so new that most people are still learning about it.

 We've poked all corners of the internet to find courses we think are smart (and often free!) These great AI classes can get anyone onto firm ground, even as this cutting-edge field gathers speed.

 Many free AI courses are available online so pick one that matches your current knowledge level. You can always move up as needed.

## [ Introduction to Generative AI Learning Path](https://www.cloudskillsboost.google/paths/118) 

![Introduction to Generative AI Learning Path homepage](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/dJve4yDxcw-UWWzP9Gi6tL7ZRC0=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/Google-Learning-Path-AI-83ec42d1c1454b2e85d2e36104c2f254.jpg) 

 What We Like

* Courses are short and ideal for micro-learners.
* Learn how Google is implementing AI in its products.
* Complete the modules and earn badges.

 What We Don't Like

* As an introductory course, it doesn't offer hands-on practice exercises.
* With it's focus on Google, other AI developments are not covered.

 A free artificial intelligence course from the search engine giant is a no-brainer. This is an introductory microlearning course that doesn't need any technical background and is a smart one to help you learn about[ generative AI](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-generative-ai-7510795) .

 The learning path begins by introducing you to Google's 7 AI principles and ends with the practice of responsible AI in Google Cloud's products.

###  Tip

 For a more technical learning path, look into Google's free[ Generative AI for Developers](https://www.cloudskillsboost.google/paths/183) course.
