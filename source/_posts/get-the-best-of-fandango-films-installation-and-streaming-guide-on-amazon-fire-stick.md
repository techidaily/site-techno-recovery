###  Setting up Your Fire Stick for Mirroring 

 Before setting up your PC, you'll need to prepare your Fire Stick for mirroring. Follow these quick steps below to set up screen mirroring on your Fire Stick:  

1. Press and hold the**Home** button on your Amazon Fire Stick until the menu appears.
2. Select**Mirroring** .
3. Your Fire Stick is now ready to receive data from your PC.

###  Setting up Your PC for Mirroring 

 Next, we will set up your PC. Follow these steps below to set up screen mirroring on your PC:  

1. From the menu bar, select the**Notifications** icon.
2. Select**Cast** . Alternatively, you can use the**WIN+K** keyboard shortcut.
3. You should see a list of your available wireless displays and audio devices.
4. Select your**Amazon Fire Stick** .

 FAQ

* How do I download Kodi on a Fire Stick?  
 To download Kodi on your Fire Stick, navigate to**Settings** and select**Device** \>**Developer Options** . Enable**Apps From Unknown Sources** . Go to the Amazon App Store and download the Downloader app. Direct the Downloader app to the Kodi website and download the 32-bit Kodi installation, and then follow the configuration prompts.
* How do I download Cinema on a Fire Stick?  
 To download the Cinema HD APK onto your Fire Stick, you'll need to sideload the app, as you can't get it from the Amazon App Store. First, download the Downloader app from the Amazon App store. On your Fire Stick, go to**Settings** \>**Device** \>**Developer Options** \>**Install Unknown Apps** . Enable Downloader, open Downloader, click on the URL field, enter**https://www.firesticktricks.com/cinema** to download the APK file for Cinema HD, and follow the prompts.
