## Unlocking EPUB Content on Your Kindle Paperwhite: Simple Steps for Enjoyment

Many Kindle users are searching for solutions to read epub on Kindle Paperwhite, because Kindle device doesn't support EPUB natively. Fortunately we've found solutions to reading epub on kindle Paperwhite.

* [Method 1\. Send epub to Kindle Paperwhite online](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/)
* [Method 2\. Convert EPUB to Kindle Supported Format](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/)

![read epub on kindle](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/read-epub-on-kindle-2019.png)

**Is EPUB compatible with Kindle?**

\--Kindle Paperwhite only supports reading books purchased from Amazon Kindle store. And these Kindle books are in their exclusive eBook formats, such as kfx, mobi, azw3, azw, etc. **Since epub is not Kindle supported ebook format, it cannot be detected in Kindle devices even if it has been transferred to Kindle via usb cable.**

\--**Most of the epub books, such as the books purchased Google Play Books, Kobo, Nook, are protected with DRM protection**, which prevent us reading epub files on non-supported readers, such as Kindle paperwhite.

Having found the reasons, the solutions will be easy to find. Since there are limitations between the device and the ebook format, why not change the device's property or the book file's format?

### Method 1\. Send ePub to Kindle Paperwhite Online

Amazon now allows us to directly send ePub format books to Kindle by web. 

Note: The epub you send to kindle should be DRM-free epub files. If your epub files are protected by drm, please skip to[method 2 to remove drm from epub books](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/) first.

Step 1\. Go to Amazon official [Send to Kindle Online](https://www.amazon.com/sendtokindle) page (make sure that you already logged into your Amazon account). 

Step 2\. Click on the yellow button "Select files from device" to upload an ePub file. 

Step 3\. Select an ePub and add to your library, send. 

![epub to kindle send](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/epub2kindle-send.png)

Step 4\. Wait for the ePub file to be sent to Kindle.

Wait until the ePub has been sent across. Once it says “In Library”, you can pick up your kindle to sync and download the newly received file. 

1\. This method only works for drm free ePub files. If your downloaded ePub books are with drm, please [remove ePub drm](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/) first.

2\. It's likely that the sent ePub files will be with the format displaying issues on your Kindle device. 

### Method 2\. Convert EPUB to Kindle Supported Format

To read ePub on Kindle Paperwhite, the best and simplest way is ebook format conversion. As we all know that Kindle only reads kindle format such as Mobi/AZW3, so how about converting epub to kindle format?

How to convert epub to Kindle ebook format? Kindle ebook converter is a must-have tool. I'm going to introduce 2 effective tools.

#### Tool 1\. Calibre

As an ebook fan, you must have heard of Calibre. This is a free ebook management tool, which helps you convert ebooks from one format to another. So you can use it to convert your epub files to Kindle format.

Download [Calibre](http://calibre-ebook.com/download), and launch it. Then add your downloaded epub books, select them and click on "Convert books" icon. Choose Mobi/AZW3 as your output format.

![add epub to calibre](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/add-epub-to-calibre.png)
