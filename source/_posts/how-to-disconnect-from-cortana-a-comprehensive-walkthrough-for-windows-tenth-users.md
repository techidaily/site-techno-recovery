## Bluetooth Speaker Connection Woes? Fix Them with This Guide for Windows Users

Bluetooth devices, such as Bluetooth mice, Bluetooth headsets, and Bluetooth speakers, are surely bringing us a lot of convenience. However, users have been reporting problems using their Bluetooth devices after they upgraded to Windows 10\.

 In this post, we will be introducing some ways to help you fix the connection issue with your Windows Bluetooth audio devices and your wireless displays.

## **Bluetooth Audio Devices**

 If you cannot see your device when pressing the Connect button in the action center, please:

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/img_58abb00734273.jpg)

**in Windows 10**

 1) Make sure that your Bluetooth is turned on both on your device and on your PC.

 On your computer, follow the path:**Start** button**\> Settings > Devices** . On the left side of the colon, choose**Bluetooth** . Then you should be able to see your Bluetooth device settings on the right side of the pane. Make sure the bar for Bluetooth is turned**On** .

 Now click**More Bluetooth options** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/img_58abb472b96e4.jpg)

 In the new opened window, make sure the box for**Allow Bluetooth devices to find this PC** is ticked.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/img_58abb4520b473.png)

 See if the Bluetooth device that you are to pair with your computer is listed here.

 2) If you cannot see the Bluetooth button here, even though you are sure that the Bluetooth is turned on on both the device and the PC, please try updating your device driver in[the following two different ways](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/) :

**in Windows 11**

 1) Make sure that your Bluetooth is turned on both on your device and on your PC.

 On your keyboard, press the**Windows logo + I keys** simultaneously to open Settings.

 From the left navigation panel, select**Bluetooth & devices** . Then make sure**Bluetooth** is toggled**On** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/win11-Bluetooth-and-devices-Bluetooth-On-1200x647.jpg)

 Click**View more devices** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/win11-Bluetooth-and-devices-View-more-devices-1200x610.jpg)

 Scroll down and click**More Bluetooth settings** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/win11-More-Bluetooth-settings-1200x647.jpg)

 Check the box for**Allow Bluetooth devices to find this PC** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/win11-Allow-Bluetooth-devices-to-find-this-PC.jpg)

 2) If you cannot see the Bluetooth button here, even though you are sure that the Bluetooth is turned on on both the device and the PC, please try updating your device driver in the following two different ways:

### **1: Update via Device Manager**
  
 Press**Windows key** and**X** at the same time, then choose**Device Manager** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/img_58abb4196f6c4.png)

 Expand category**Bluetooth** , then right click the device you have and choose**Update Driver Software** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/img_58abb80fad061.jpg)

 Then choose**Search automatically for updated driver software** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/img_58abb8274a110.jpg)

## **Miracast Devices**

 Miracast is like a wireless HDMI cable that enables your devices to discover each other, connect, and mirror the contents of their screens wirelessly.

 If you cannot see your device when pressing the Connect button in the action center, please:

1) Make sure your Windows device supports Miracast.

2) Make sure that your Wi-Fi is on.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/img_58abdc277f53f.png)

## **PCs**

 If you cannot see your PC when pressing the Connect button in the action center, please:

1) Make sure your Wi-Fi is on.

2) Make sure your PC supports Miracast and it is plugged in.

3) Go to**Settings > System > Projecting to this PC** and make sure that your PC is set up to be discovered.
